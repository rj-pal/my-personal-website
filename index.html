Bio

I am passionate about languages including both human and machine. I work with symbols and data to produce artifacts. I embarked on this remarkable journey of teaching after finishing my studies in mathematics and philosophy in Guelph, Ontario. This first took me to the Lower Mainland where I discovered the power of unlocking potential. The journey continued on to the Island of Japan where I fully embraced the art of helping others. I stayed in Japan for nearly a decade and a half.

I had a successul career as a Native English Language Instructor. I worked for Aeon Central Japan, located in Nagoya City, Aichi Prefecture. I was the Senior Instructor at the Head School in Nagoya where students could experience studying abroad in Japan in an intensive full-time program. Students were immersed in mastering English communication building their 4-Skills using techniques like Content-Based Learning and Communicative Learning under the umberage of one of Japan's leading private English Learning companyies, now operated by tech giant KDDI. I left my position to return home to be closer to my family and start my own tribe. Sadly, the in-person school I hold dear to my heart closed due to the shift to online learning after the Covid pandaemic swept the world.

Upon my return to Canada, I took up coding and learning the fundamentals of Computer and Data Science in my transition from life abroad. My coding journey continues today.

Until the recent reduction in the number of international students, I worked for the School of Business at Conestoga College teaching Business Research, Data Analysis and Business Mathematics.

I am a life-long learner currently honing my coding skills while looking to make a carreer transition. Contact me here if you want to talk
